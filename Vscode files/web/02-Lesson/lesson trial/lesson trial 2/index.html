Late Prof.(Mrs) Dora Nkem Akunyili

Director-General of N.A.F.D.A.C. (A woman known for her integrity and commitment in the Eradication of Fake drugs

Here's a time line of Late Prof. Dora Akunyili's life:

  1. 1954: Born in Makurdi, Benue State, Nigeria on the 14th of July to Chief & Mrs Paul Young Edemobi.
  2. 1966-1973: Finished her primary and secondary school education.
  3. 1978-1981: Obtained her first degree in Pharmacology (B.Pham) at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka (UNN) and worked as a pharmacist at the University of Nigeria Teaching Hospital (UNTH) Enugu..
  4. 1985-1992: Obtained her PhD and Lectured at UNN.
  5. 1992-1995: Worked as a part-time lecturer at the west African postgraduate School of pharmacists Lagos, Nigeria.
  6. 1996-2001: Worked as a consultant pharmacologist in the Faculty of Medicine, UNN.
  7. 2001: Appointed Director General of the National Agency for Food and Drugs Administration and Control (NAFDAC). Immediately launched an all-out attack against the manufacture and distribution of fake drugs in Nigeria.
  8. 2006-2008: Appointed Chairman of West African Drug Regulatory Agencies Network (WADRAN) in March and Vice Chairman of the International Medical Products Anti-Counterfeiting Task Force (IMPACT) in November.
  9. 2008-2010: Served as Minister of Information and Communication
  10. 2014: Died in an India Hospital on June 7 after a long battle with cancer at the age of 59 years. Laid to rest in Agulu in Anambra State in August.
